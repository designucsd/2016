Spaces

 

H&SS 1346

H&SS 1346 is our hangout and our event space. It’s a communal design space for students to collaborate on projects. The space is equipped with moveable furniture, prototyping materials, and small break-out rooms, making it a great space for collaborative design projects. If you’re a student in a design class lucky enough to have access to the room, it's your responsibility to help maintain it.

Where is it?

The Design Lab

We sometimes host talks and workshops over at the Design Lab in Atkinson Hall. The Design Lab is an organization on campus whose goal is to combine practice with theory and make UCSD a leader in design theory. They have many ongoing research projects, superb faculty and staff, and a team of undergraduate research assistants. As generous sponsors of Design at UCSD, they provide guidance, funding, and allow us to use their space.
