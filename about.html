Aspiring Designers


Our story.


We’re a student-led group of designers focusing on UX design, research, and design thinking, but we’ve got an appreciation for all design fields and related disciplines. We’re your springboard into a design career. We support you by providing the resources, know-how, and the community to get you the design job you want -- you bring the drive to make it happen. (And don’t forget to bring a healthy appetite for our Taco Tuesdays hangouts.)
